What is Fund Management on Forex?

The Fund Management that we provide is implemented through percent allocation management module which allows Investors to have their accounts managed by a trader appointed by them on the basis of a limited trading power of attorney. This technical solution allows the Master trader on one trading platform to manage simultaneously unlimited quantity of Investor managed accounts. Depending on the size of the deposit each Investor`s managed account has its own ratio in all pool. Total sum of all ratios under one pool is always equal to 100%. Trader's activity results (trades, Profit & Loss) are allocated between Investor`s managed accounts according to ratio.
